This is a basic Inventory Control System 

You can View the current inventory.
You can Add items with price and quantity to the inventory.
You can delete items from the inventory (a record of deletion will be added to the inventory)

After Viewing the inventory Please use the Back button to return to the main page.
After Adding or Deleting an item you will be automatically redirected to the SUCCESS page and may continue to edit or view the inventory.
